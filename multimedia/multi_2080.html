Tribhuvan University

Institute of Science and Technology

2080

Bachelor Level / elective-i-semester / Science

Computer Science and Information Technology( CSC319 )

Multimedia Computing

Full Marks: 60 + 20 + 20

Pass Marks: 24 + 8 + 8

Time: 3 Hours

Candidates are required to give their answers in their own words as far as practicable.

The figures in the margin indicate full marks.

SECTION A

Attempt any TWO questions.

1

Explain the global structure of multimedia with block diagram.

2

Explain the JPEG compression process steps in detail with example.

3

What do you mean by Huffman coding? Explain the Huffman coding process with example.

SECTION B

Attempt any EIGHT questions.

4

Explain the properties of multimedia computing.

5

Difference between bitmap and vector graphics.

6

Explain the computer based animation.

7

How speech is generated? Describe.

8

What are the multimedia interface component?

9

Discuss the animation language.

10

Explain the abstraction levels of programming.

11

Explain the application of multimedia in media integration.

12

Explain the digital image representation with example.
